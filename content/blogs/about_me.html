Biography of Emilia Moskala

Early life

My name is Emilia Moskala and I was born in Poland on 30.07.1999. My parents, Izabella and Jacek, lived in a small town in southern Poland - Kalwaria Zebrzydowska. That’s where I spent the most of my childhood. After I graduated local primary school, me and my family moved to another city, Krakow, so that I can continue my secondary education.

Education

I continued my education at August Witkowski Vth High School in Krakow, where I focused on the following majors:

  1. Mathematics,
  2. Physics,
  3. IT.

After graduating High School, I enrolled Warsaw School of Economics, the best economic university in Poland. I pursued Quantitative Methods in Economics and Information Systems programme, where I had an opportunity to take up courses elaborating on applications of IT in enterprises.

Business Background

During my studies, I applied for Deloitte’s IT Academy, where I had an opportunity to learn about information systems, integration, IT architecture, and data warehouse. Headhunted by recruiters, I joined Technology Strategy & Transformation at Deloitte Digital.

London Business School

Through my experience at Deloitte, I realised that I would like to deepen my knowledge on the opportunities posed by digital disruption, and I found MAM curriculum perfectly tailored not only for my professional but also academic interests. Therefore, I decided to continue my education at LBS.
